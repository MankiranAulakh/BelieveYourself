GD Coursework

About the project

These artworks are from my GD157 course which I took in Winter 2023. I developed my skills to use Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop.

How was the project completed

I did a variety of assignments in this course including creating shapes and patterns, thumbnails, image editting and masking.

I created a poster on an Indian Festival (Diwali) for my final project. You can also view the mock-up of the poster on its right-hand side.

Tools used

I used Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop to create all the coursework.

What was the result

I completed all my coursework on time and received A grade at the end of the course.
