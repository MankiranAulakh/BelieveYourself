UFV ITS Marketing Material

About the project

While working as Communications Specialist Co-op student, I was involved in creating and designing content to communicate services offered by the IT department.

How was the project completed

I designed bookmarks for Eduroam Wi-Fi and multi-factor authentication. I also created poster for Eduroam Wi-Fi. I designed poster to communicate ITS social media accounts, and also ITS business card to be placed at IT Service Desk.

Tools used

I mainly used Canva to create all the communication and marketing material.

What was the result

My boss really liked the design of the marketing material. Bookmarks and business cards are placed in the IT Service Desk for students and employees. Posters are also on the bulletin boards throughout the UFV campus.
